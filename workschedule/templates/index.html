Cloud Your Schedule in 4 Steps

1. Download Your Schedule

Go to your schedule page on the Workforce app. Click the download icon. Choose the date range you want to download. Click, SAVE.

2. Upload to Our Site

Upload the pdf from Workforce, usually in the Downloads folder. Click the Upload Schedule button for a walk through.

Upload Schedule

3. Check Our Work

We'll send you an email with the extracted data for a quick review and edit. Reply with your changes, and acceptance.

4. Sync to Calendar

We'll send you a final email with a button that will add the schedule to your calendar. Click it and your calendar is refreshed automatically with the new entries.
