Cloud Your Schedule in 4 Steps

1. Download Your Schedule

Go to your online schedule. Click the download icon. Choose the date range you want to download. Click SAVE. Then download the PDF to your Downloads folder.

2. Upload To Our Site

Upload the PDF you downloaded. Hit the Upload Schedule button below, for a walk through.

3. Check Our Work

We'll display a list of the shifts in your schedule for your review. Look closely. Each entry will be a separate event in your calendar. If the schedule looks good, hit the Accept and Pay button.
